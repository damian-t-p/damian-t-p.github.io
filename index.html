I'm Damian, and I'm a postdoctoral researcher in statistics at the Burnet Institute in Melbourne Australia. I completed my PhD at Stanford's Statistics department in 2022, where I was advised by Iain Johnstone.

I am interested in applying modern statistical methods to data in the public health domain. I work on diverse projects, including the use of high-dimenstional statistics in the proteomics of people with HIV and longitudinal causal inference in long-term studies of housing and incarceration.
